guidance: "The Community Infrastructure Levy (CIL) regulations require all local planning
  authorities that \nissue a CIL liability notice or enter into section 106 planning
  obligations during a reporting year \nto publish an infrastructure funding statement
  (IFS) at least annually. \nThe infrastructure funding statement should, as a minimum,
  include the information set out in \n[Schedule 2 to the Community Infrastructure
  Levy regulations 2010](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukdsi/2010/9780111492390/contents)\n(as
  inserted by the 2019 regulations).\n\nIn addition, it is recommended that local
  authorities also publish the raw data that informed the infrastructure funding statement.\nThis
  guidance sets out a data format and approach that you can follow in order to publish
  data that sits behind the infrastructure statement.\n\nThe data format and infrastructure
  funding statements will give policy makers and communities better insight into how
  developer contributions\nare supporting new development and local infrastructure.\nWe
  have designed the data format to cause as little disruption as possible to existing
  processes.\n"
